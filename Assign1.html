MIT PUNE

MIT Pune offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, with a focus on innovation and research. Known for its strong engineering and management courses, the campus is equipped with modern facilities and vibrant student life.

Symbiosis International University

Symbiosis International University is a multi-disciplinary university offering a range of courses in arts, science, commerce, law, and more. The university is renowned for its diverse cultural environment and extensive global collaborations.

College of Engineering Pune

COEP College, one of the oldest and most prestigious colleges in Pune, offers a rich blend of tradition and modern education. The college is known for its excellent faculty, wide range of programs, and active student community.
